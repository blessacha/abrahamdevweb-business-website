    Stages for Web/App Design & Development

    INITIATION

    Before we start building a website or mobile application, we need to gather essential information. This includes your purpose, primary goals, target audience, etc.

    PLANNING

    Once we have gathered the necessary information for your website or mobile application, we start by creating a sitemap.

    EXECUTION

    In this phase, we will design the layout, carry out the development work, do the testing, and ensure you are satisfied with the quality standards.

    MONITORING & CONTROL

    You have got any new ideas and you want us to make the update on your website or mobile application as soon as possible? Oh yes, we are always here to help you. Let us know and get the development done as soon as possible.

    CLOSING

    Submit the project for review and listen to feedback, complete minor issues or updates, resubmit project for more feedback, listen to feedback ensure it meets your requirement and satisfaction! Publish project. Hand over all the project data to the owner and end contract.
